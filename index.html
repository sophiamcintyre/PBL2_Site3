Course Materials

Find everything you need for your classes at Cardinal Books. We stock required textbooks, lab materials, art supplies, and more for every Ball State course.

  1. Visit our online course materials lookup tool
  2. Enter your course department and number (e.g., ENGL 103)
  3. View required and recommended materials for your section
  4. Choose from new, used, rental, or digital options
  5. Order online for pickup or have items shipped to you
